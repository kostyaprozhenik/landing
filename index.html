Account Management Advanced

Program Goal

The program is aimed at building & enhancing the following skills among senior AMs:
consultancy mindset and systems thinking, facilitating the Client’s long-term digital
transformation programs, identifying the Client’s business pain points and aligning
Client’s strategic vision and understanding of added value with that of EPAM.

Upon completion of this program participants
should be able to:

  • Integrate key components of the consultative/Trusted Advisor approach into their client relationship management practice.
  • Prioritize own efforts to maximize EPAM’s and a Client’s value (effectively apply value mapping approach to account planning).
  • Suggest approaches of how to address competing demands of EPAM external and internal stakeholders.
  • Recognize change when it comes and identify ways to deal with it properly and timely on three levels: project scope, implicit delivery model, internal changes.
  • Apply multi-level risks management techniques to identify, prioritize, mitigate, and report account, program, and project risks.
  • Keep track of strategic performance indicators and metrics to report to our clients.
  • Diagnoze Client’s readiness to digital transformation and guide them on the next steps from establishing relevant building blocks to digital strategy planning and implementation.
  • Identify complex client engagements and bringing the expertise of EPAM’s value creation network to the service of our clients.
  • Apply expert toolkit of the most skilled and successful negotiators, who serve as a Trusted Advisor/Consultant, in the following cases: negotiating commercial terms, MSA negotiations for new logos and managing negotiations over the most difficult terms in the MSA, re-negotiating higher staff rates for the relocated employees.

Learning Journey

During the program participants complete self-study materials and practice key skills and approaches during practical training sessions
